How every claim on this site is labelled
requirement
An agreed use case, scenario or target (ITU-R IMT-2030, TR 22.870, TR 38.914). Sourced and dated.
candidate
A technology option under study — waveforms, sub-THz, AI-native, RIS, ISAC. Shown neutrally.
foundation
An established 5G fundamental 6G builds on — taught concretely in the Foundations course.
